Welcome to Royal Friends Gummadidala

Royal Friends Gummadidala is a youth-driven social service group formed with the aim of serving society, supporting the needy, and bringing positive change to our village and surrounding areas.

Our Vision

To build a responsible, united, and compassionate youth community that actively contributes to social welfare and community development.

Our Mission

Our mission is to encourage social responsibility among youth and actively participate in service-oriented activities such as helping underprivileged families, supporting community needs, and promoting cleanliness and environmental awareness.
