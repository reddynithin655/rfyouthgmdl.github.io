RF Youth GMDL is a youth-led organization formed with the aim of serving society and supporting people in need. Our group believes that youth have the power to bring positive change through unity, responsibility, and compassion.

We actively participate in social service activities such as awareness programs, community support initiatives, cleanliness drives, and volunteer-based services.

Our goal is to inspire young people to contribute their time and energy towards building a better and more responsible society.
