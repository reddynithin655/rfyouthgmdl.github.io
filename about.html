About Us

Royal Friends Gummadidala is a youth-driven group based in the village of Gummadidala. Our group was formed with the purpose of serving society and bringing positive change through unity and social responsibility.

We believe that youth have the strength and energy to support community development, help people in need, and inspire others through action.

Through various social service activities, we aim to create awareness, support underprivileged individuals, and contribute to the overall well-being of society.
